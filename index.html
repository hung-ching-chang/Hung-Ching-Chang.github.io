About Me

I am a PhD student in Biostatistics at Graduate School of Public Health, University of Pittsburgh under the guidance of Dr. George C. Tseng and Dr. Kayhan Batmanghelich. I am also a graduate student researcher under the guidance of Dr. Nader Shaikh at UPMC Children's Hospital of Pittsburgh.

I received my M.S. degree in Biostatistics at the Institute of Epidemiology and Preventive Medicine, National Taiwan University (NTU) in 2019. During my Master's year, I joined in Chuhsing Kate Hsiao Lab and visited research group Mathematics in Life Science at Freie Universität Berlin under Dr. Alexander Bockmayr’s supervision.
